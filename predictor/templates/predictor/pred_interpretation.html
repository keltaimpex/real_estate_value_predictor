Tips for Interpreting Predictions

Understanding your house price prediction is crucial. Here are some tips to help you interpret the results:

  • The predicted price is an estimate based on the input features (e.g., size, location).
  • Higher-grade houses and larger houses generally have higher prices.
  • Location (latitude and longitude) also plays a significant role in determining house prices.
  • If your prediction seems higher or lower than expected, consider adjusting the input features and trying again.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How accurate is the prediction?

The accuracy depends on the quality and relevance of the input data. While this model is designed to provide a reliable estimate, I recommend cross-referencing with market trends and expert opinions.

2. Can I trust the model for all house types?

The model works best with standard residential properties. Unique properties (e.g., very large estates, highly customized homes) might not be as accurately predicted.

3. Why does the model need information about the neighborhood?

Neighborhood factors (like location, proximity to amenities, etc.) heavily influence house prices. My model uses these to ensure accurate predictions.

4. How can I improve the accuracy of my prediction?

Try to provide accurate and detailed information about the house's features, including square footage, number of rooms, and age of the property.
